Kick RoX — Privacy Policy

Effective: May 3, 2026  |  App: Kick RoX  |  NOLA ESK8 Tracker

Kick RoX is an electric skateboard ride tracker built for the New Orleans ESK8 community. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we never do with it.

1. What We Collect

Kick RoX stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Ride logs — distance, speed, duration, date/time
  • Pre-ride checklists — your gear check responses
  • Chat messages — squad chat stored locally
  • ICE profile — blood type, emergency contact, allergies (you enter this yourself)
  • App preferences — settings and display options

No account. No login. No server sync. Everything stays on your phone/tablet.

2. What We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Location / GPS data — Kick RoX displays a static map placeholder. We do not access, store, or transmit your GPS coordinates.
  • ❌ Personal identifiers — no name, email, phone number, or device ID is ever collected.
  • ❌ Analytics — no tracking pixels, no usage stats sent anywhere.
  • ❌ Crash reports — no third-party crash handlers.
  • ❌ Third-party SDKs with data collection — we use React and Tailwind CSS from CDN. These are framework libraries, not data collectors.

3. Fall Detection / Emergency Alerts

The app includes a fall detection feature. If triggered, it shows a countdown and can fire an alert. In the current build, the alert is simulated — no actual SMS or GPS location is sent. In a production build with a real backend, you would control which number receives the alert.

You can abort the alert at any time by tapping ABORT.

4. Hazards & Squad Chat

Hazards reported in the app and squad chat messages are stored locally in your browser/app storage. We do not have a server, so reports stay on your device. Future versions may add optional cloud sync — at which point this policy will be updated with clear opt-in controls.

5. Google Play & App Store Requirements

This app is published via Google Play. Google may collect standard Play Store data (installs, crashes) as per Google's Privacy Policy. This is outside our control — it's Google's standard app distribution data.

6. Cookies & Third Parties

Kick RoX uses one third-party CDN (jsdelivr.net) to load React and Tailwind. These are trusted framework files with their own terms of service. We do not embed ads, tracking scripts, or analytics.

7. Data Retention

All data is stored in your browser's local storage or app cache. You can delete all data at any time by clearing your browser/app data, or by uninstalling the app. We do not maintain any server-side copy.

8. Children's Privacy

Kick RoX is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we add cloud features or change how data is handled, we'll update this policy and note the change with the effective date.
